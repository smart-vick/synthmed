### **SECTION 9: FAQ**

**Q1: Is this real patient data?**
```
No—100% synthetic and generated. There is zero real PII (Personally
Identifiable Information). Every field is synthetic, including names,
addresses, dates, and medical records. You can use it anywhere without
privacy concerns.
```

**Q2: Can I use this in production?**
```
Absolutely. SynthMed is designed specifically for production ML pipelines.
Hospitals, startups, and universities use our data in production systems
daily. The data is clinically coherent and statistically realistic.
```

**Q3: Do I need legal approval?**
```
No. Since it's 100% synthetic, it doesn't fall under privacy regulations
like PIPEDA or HIPAA. No legal review needed. Deploy immediately.
```

**Q4: How is this different from random fake data?**
```
Real medical knowledge. Our generator understands clinical correlations:
- Patients with high glucose have high HbA1c
- Diabetic medications match diagnoses
- Blood pressure reflects comorbidities
- Age-appropriate disease distribution

Generic fake data fails these checks immediately.
```

**Q5: Why is it so much cheaper than competitors?**
```
Three reasons:

3. API-first design (lower support overhead)

Competitors try to be everything to everyone. We're focused and efficient.
```
