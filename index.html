FAQ

Common questions

Answers to what healthcare AI teams ask before their first order.

Is this data actually compliant with PIPEDA?
SynthMed data contains no personal information. Because it is entirely statistically generated and does not derive from real patient records, it falls outside PIPEDA's scope. However, we always recommend consulting your institutional privacy officer for your specific regulatory situation. We provide a methodology document with every order to support your compliance discussions.
How is the data generated?
We use probabilistic models built from published epidemiological statistics and population health benchmarks — not individual health records. Diagnoses, medications, and lab values are modeled to co-vary realistically using clinical literature and Canadian health information standards.
What formats do you deliver in?
Starter: CSV and JSON. Professional: CSV, JSON, and Parquet. Enterprise: all formats plus custom schema delivery. All orders include a methodology summary PDF.
Can I get custom fields or condition distributions?
Yes — on Professional and Enterprise orders, you can request specific condition prevalence rates, age distributions, field additions, and regional concentrations. Contact us before ordering to discuss your requirements. We typically respond within one business day.
Do you offer a free sample?
Yes. Request a free 1,000-record sample using the contact form. We'll deliver it within 24 hours at no cost and with no obligation. This lets you validate field structure, data realism, and format compatibility before purchasing.
What's your refund policy?
If your dataset doesn't match your agreed specifications, we'll regenerate it at no additional charge. If we're unable to meet your requirements, we offer a full refund. We recommend requesting the free sample first to verify fit before committing to a paid order.

Synthetic data is a powerful tool for development and testing. It is not a replacement for real clinical data in all contexts. We're transparent about that.

Data Security & Compliance

Last updated: January 2026

Our Core Position

SynthMed's fundamental security advantage is architectural: we never collect, store, or process real patient data. There is no patient data to breach.

Generation Security

Synthetic datasets are generated using statistical models that run in isolated compute environments. No real patient records are used as inputs. Generation logs do not contain any personal health information.

Delivery Security

Completed datasets are delivered via time-limited, authenticated download links. Links expire after 72 hours. Files are delivered as compressed archives.

Contact Information Handling

Contact form submissions are transmitted over TLS and stored in access-controlled environments. Access is limited to SynthMed team members with a legitimate need. We do not use third-party CRM platforms for initial contact handling.

Compliance Framework

Because our data contains no personal information, it falls outside the scope of PIPEDA's personal information provisions. We design our processes to align with PIPEDA's fair information principles as a matter of best practice regardless.

Reporting a Concern

If you have a security concern or question, contact vk800772@gmail.com. We will respond within 2 business days.
